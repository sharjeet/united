About

Who I am and what I do

Hello, My name is Nirban Karmakar,the owner of United Photography. I am from Kolkata, India and have been working since 2015 with my team, where we covered more than 150 weddings. With a fly in the wall approach to our shooting, we strive to capture the most candid. yet memorable moments in the hope of telling a wedding story that not only reflects the larger than life feeling of an Indian Wedding but also documents those little unseen fleeting moments that a couple and their family would experience in all their hurry and chaotic bliss hoping to give them a chance to relive the day's experience in its complete glory. We also strive to make every moment of your special day memorable for you, keeping it fresh for years.
